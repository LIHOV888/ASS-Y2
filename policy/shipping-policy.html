Shipping Policy

Fast, Free Shipping on All Orders

Free Shipping

We offer FREE 2-day shipping on all orders within the continental United States. No minimum purchase required. All orders are shipped via FedEx, UPS, or USPS with tracking information provided.

Processing Time

  • Standard Orders: Processed within 1-2 business days
  • Custom PCs: Built and shipped within 3-5 business days
  • Pre-orders: Ship on or before the release date

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard (USPS/UPS Ground) 2-5 business days FREE
Expedited (UPS 2nd Day) 2 business days $25
Express (UPS Next Day) 1 business day $45

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination:

  • Canada: 3-7 business days, $25 flat rate
  • Europe: 5-10 business days, $45 flat rate
  • Asia & Australia: 7-14 business days, $55 flat rate

Note: International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country.

Order Tracking

All orders include tracking information. You'll receive an email with tracking details once your order ships. Track your package at:

  • FedEx: fedex.com
  • UPS: ups.com
  • USPS: usps.com

Signature Requirements

  • Orders under $500: No signature required
  • Orders $500-$1000: Signature required
  • Orders over $1000: Adult signature required

Packaging

All computers are shipped in custom foam-injected boxes designed specifically for safe transport. Components are packaged in anti-static bags with protective foam inserts. Every package includes:

  • Original product packaging
  • All accessories and documentation
  • Quick start guide
  • Warranty information

Delivery Issues

If your package is lost, damaged, or delayed:

  1. Check tracking information for delivery status
  2. Contact the carrier directly with tracking number
  3. If issue persists, contact our support team at support@pcstore.com

Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to:

  • PO Boxes (for large items like computers)
  • APO/FPO addresses
  • Certain remote locations (contact us for exceptions)
